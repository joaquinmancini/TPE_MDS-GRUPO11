Welcome to the online home of RecoleCoop

RecoleCoop is the online center for the best of recolection, recycle, environment, green. RecoleCoop was founded with the idea of being able to give collectors, citizens the best recolection, recycle, environment, green possible. RecoleCoop is a Cooperative created to help collectors from Tandil, with the intention to group them and make them stronger. It also aims to create consciousness about taking care of our environment. At RecoleCoop we are dedicated to:

  • Group collectors
  • Recycle as much as posible
  • Create consciousness about environment

¿Cómo entrego los materiales?

Para poder reciclar los envases que consumimos habitualmente, primero debemos separarlos en el contenedor correspondiente según el material del que están hechos. Así, los envases de plástico, latas y briks van al contenedor amarillo y los envases de papel y cartón al azul.
